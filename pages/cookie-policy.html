Cookie Policy

Effective date: November 1, 2025

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help the site remember information about your visit (like language, preferences or login status).

How we use cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functions and form submissions.
  • Performance & analytics: To understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics) so we can improve content and experience.
  • Functional cookies: Save preferences and provide a smoother experience.

Third-party cookies

We may use trusted third-party services (for example analytics and social sharing). These third parties may set cookies. We do not control third-party cookie policies — please check their websites for details.

Your choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality. To opt out of analytics tracking, you can use browser add-ons or Google’s opt-out tools.

Managing cookies on the site

If you deploy cookies/analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), we recommend adding a cookie consent banner for visitors in jurisdictions that require explicit consent. This page serves as the policy details linked from that banner.
